REZAEI

Conceptual architect Shervin Sheikh Rezaei, currently based in Ghent, channels her personal chaos into structural order through her profession. Designing things of her own will and solidly sealing objects has always acted as a necessary meditation for her. Sheikh Rezaei works as an architect for Jan De Vylder and Inge Vinck, and her thesis ‘ENSEMBLE: An architecture of the inbetween’ will be released as a book by publisher MER this autumn. From this year, she will also be teaching Mixed Media at LUCA School of Arts Interior. No matter what the future holds, she wants to keep on dreaming, creating and exploring new experiences.
